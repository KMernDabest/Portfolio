About me

I am a recent Software Engineering graduate from the Cambodia Academy of Digital Technology (CADT), driven by a passion for creating technology that is not only powerful but also accessible and delightful to use.

My journey in tech has given me a strong foundation in multiple programming languages and frameworks, allowing me to thrive on both the front-end and back-end. However, I discovered my true calling lies at the intersection of code and design. I believe that exceptional software is born from a seamless marriage of clean, efficient logic and thoughtful, user-centered design principles (UX/UI). I am adept at translating complex problems into intuitive interfaces and am continuously exploring ways to enhance user satisfaction and engagement.

I am proficient in JavaScript (React, Node.js), Java, and SQL, and I'm always eager to learn new technologies and methodologies.
